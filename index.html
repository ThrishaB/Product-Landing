History

For as long as she can remember, every time she visited her grandmother, she watched her knit various things such as hats, dresses and sweaters while her grandmother tells her of stories from her grandmother's past. She watches as her grandmother's hands worked quickly and steadily. Her grandmother's work were not like any other as the patterns were very intricate. She has always been fascinated of how someone could turn such a simple item into something completely different. At her younger years, she merely watched her grandmother worked but as she grew older, she wanted to learn how so fifteen years ago, a teenage Kat Reyes asked her grandmother to teach her how to knit. Along the way, she decided to learn much more advanced techniques than what her grandmother taught her which in turn lead to her friends and family praising her magnificent work. Now a grown woman, she has turned the hobby of knitting into a blooming business.
